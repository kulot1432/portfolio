Favorite Series

One Peice

One Piece is an epic pirate adventure manga and anime series that follows the journey of Monkey D. Luffy and his colorful crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, as they sail the Grand Line in search of the legendary treasure, the One Piece. With its vibrant world-building, unforgettable characters, and thrilling adventures, One Piece has captured the hearts of millions worldwide. The series is renowned for its epic battles, heartfelt moments, and a touch of humor, making it a beloved classic among anime fans.

The Legend of the Blue Sea

The Legend of the Blue Sea is a captivating love story between a cunning con artist and a naive mermaid, blending fantasy and romance in a heartwarming and humorous tale.

Bad Buddy Series

Bad Buddy is a heartwarming Thai BL series that follows the story of two lifelong rivals, Pran and Pat. Despite their constant bickering and competitive nature, they gradually develop feelings for each other, leading to a beautiful and complex love story. The series is praised for its realistic portrayal of young love, its well-developed characters, and its stunning cinematography. It's a must-watch for fans of the BL genre and anyone who appreciates a good love story.
